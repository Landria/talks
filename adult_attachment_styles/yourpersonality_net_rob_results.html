Viewing Your Results
On this page you can view your results from the personality survey(s) you have taken. Use the pull-down menu above to select the results you would like to see. If you have only taken one survey, then you will only have the option to see your latest results. If you have taken the surveys on more than one occasion, you will also be able to see your results across time. This kind of output is useful for seeing whether and how your personality has evolved across time.
